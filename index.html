Career: Doctor

So you want to become a doctor... it's a long journey

How to become a doctor

High School: Achieve strong academic performance.

Undergraduate Studies: Complete a bachelor's degree (typically 4 years) with prerequisite courses for medical school.

Medical School: Attend and graduate from medical school (4 years).

Residency: Complete a residency program (3-9 years) in your chosen specialty.

Fellowship (Optional): For subspecialization, complete a fellowship (1-3 years).

Licensure: Obtain a medical license from the state where you plan to practice.
